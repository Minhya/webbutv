JENNIE

JENNIE, born on January 16, 1996, in Seoul, South Korea. JENNIE is a South Korean singer and rapper known for her charismatic stage presence and versatile talent.

Other than her record-breaking career with Blackpink, she became the first Korean female solo artist to top the iTunes Worldwide Song Chart with her solo single “Solo” in 2018, hit No. 1 on Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart, and earned numerous accolades as well.

SOLO

Released on November 12, 2018, "SOLO" is Jennie's debut single.

You & Me

Released on October 6, 2023, "You & Me" is Jennie's second solo single.

JENNIE

Released on March 7, 2025, "JENNIE" is Jennie's debut solo album.
