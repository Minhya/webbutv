JISOO

JISOO, born on January 3, 1995, in Gunpo, South Korea. JISOO, who debuted with BLACKPINK in 2016, is a multi-talented artist, shining both as a singer and actress.

She is recognized not only for her contributions to the group but also for her successful solo career, earning multiple awards in music and acting.
