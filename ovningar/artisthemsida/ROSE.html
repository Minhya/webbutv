ROSÉ

ROSÉ, born on February 11, 1997, in Auckland, New Zealand, and raised in Melbourne, Australia. ROSÉ is a South Korean singer and dancer known for her unique voice and emotional performances.

Rosé, a member of the K-pop group Blackpink, has achieved significant success as a solo artist, particularly with her debut album "Rosie" and the hit single "Apt." featuring Bruno Mars, which made her the first female K-pop artist to reach the top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100. She has also broken multiple records, including the fastest K-pop track to reach 1 billion streams on Spotify.
